I Northwestern IACMAC/ISC conference

“Nosocomial infections: prevention, diagnosis, and treatment”

September 14-15, 2006, Saint Petersburg, Russia

Intro | Programme

On behalf of IACMAC and ISC we have a pleasure to invite you to attend I Northwestern conference “Nosocomial Infections: Prophylaxis, Diagnosis, and Treatment” which will be held on 14-15 September, 2006 in Saint Petersburg, Russia.

Venue: Meeting Hall of the Military Medial Academy

Address: Saint Petersburg, B. Sampsonievsky prospect, 1.

How to get there: Metro station “Lenin Square”; trolley routes ## 3, 8, 12, 38, 43, trams ## 6, 20, 30, 38, 61, 63, 90, buses: ## 106, 107, 13.

Conference organizers:

  • Inter-Regional Association for clinical microbiology and antimicrobial chemotherapy (IACMAC, Russia)
  • International Society for Chemotherapy Infection and Cancer (ISC)
  • Federal Agency on Healthcare and Social Development
  • Saint Petersburg Medical Academy of Postgraduate Education
  • Russian Academy of Medical Sciences
  • Institute of Antimicrobial Chemotherapy (IAC, Smolensk, Russia)

A colloquium “Antimicrobial susceptibility testing of nosocomial pathogens” will be held on 15th September, 2006.

Conference official language: Russian

Registration: free for medical professionals

Looking forward to welcome you in Saint Petersburg in September!
