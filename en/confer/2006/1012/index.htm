III Far East IACMAC/ISC/ESCMID
Conference on Antimicrobial Therapy

October 12-13, 2006, Khabarovsk, Russia

Intro | Programme

On behalf of IACMAC, ISC and ESCMID we have a pleasure to invite you to attend III Far East Conference on Antimicrobial Therapy which will be held on 12-13 October, 2006 in Khabarovsk, Russia.

Venue: Conference Hall of the Institute of Postgraduate Education of Healthcare Professionals of the Ministry of Health of Khabarovsk region.

Address: Khabarovsk, Krasnodarskay str., 9.

Conference organizers:

  • Inter-Regional Association for clinical microbiology and antimicrobial chemotherapy (IACMAC, Russia)
  • International Society for Chemotherapy Infection and Cancer (ISC)
  • European Society for Clinical Microbiology and Antimicrobial Therapy (ESCMID)
  • Institute of Postgraduate Education of Healthcare Professionals of the Ministry of Health of Khabarovsk region
  • Russian Academy of Medical Sciences
  • Institute of Antimicrobial Chemotherapy (IAC, Smolensk, Russia)

A colloquium for microbiologists will be held on 13th October, 2006.

Conference official language: Russian

Registration: free for medical professionals

Looking forward to welcome you in Khabarovsk in October!
